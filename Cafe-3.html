Crafting Memorable Experiences Since 2018

Nestled in the heart of Thane, Brew & Bites was born from a passion for exceptional coffee and a desire to create a space where people could connect, create, and unwind. Our founders, coffee enthusiasts with years of experience, wanted to bring specialty coffee culture to our vibrant city.

We source our beans directly from ethical farms, roast them to perfection in-house, and craft each cup with precision. Our food menu complements our beverages with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients prepared with care.
